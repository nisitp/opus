The Bank of Israel is the central bank of the State of Israel.

Key Facts
The Bank of Israel's Banking Supervision Department supervises the stability of banking corporations with a view to protecting depositors' money; ensures proper conduct of banking corporations; assures fair business relations between banks and their customers.
