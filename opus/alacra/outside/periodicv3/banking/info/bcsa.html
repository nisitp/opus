The British Columbia Securities Commission (BCSC) is the independent provincial government agency responsible for regulating securities trading in British Columbia through the administration of the Securities Act.

Key Facts
Securities market participants fund the BCSA operations through fees they pay under the Securities Act.

Additional Information
British Columbia Securities Act

Who it affects
All persons or entities trading in or advising on a security or exchange contract.
