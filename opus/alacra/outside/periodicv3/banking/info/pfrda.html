The Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA) is the prudential regulator for the pensions sector in India, including the National Pension System.

Key Facts
The mandate of PFRDA is development and regulation of the pension sector in India as "an authority to promote old age income security by establishing, developing and regulating pension funds, to protect the interest of subscribers to schemes of pension funds and for matters connected therewith or incidental therto."
