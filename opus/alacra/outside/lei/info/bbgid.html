Definition
The Bloomberg Global ID, or BBGID, is a 12-digit alpha-numeric, randomly generated ID covering active and non-active securities.

Additional Information
Securities to which BBGIDs can be issued include common stock, options, derivatives, futures, corporate and government bonds, municipals, currencies, and mortgage products. Unique BBGIDs identify securities as well as individual exchanges on which they trade. Composite BBGIDs are also issued to represent unique securities across related exchanges.
