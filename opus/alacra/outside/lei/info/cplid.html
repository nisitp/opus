Definition
CounterpartyLink was formed in 2005 with the sole remit to collect, maintain and deliver accurate and auditable legal entity reference data from around the world.

Additional Information
The data provided by CounterpartyLink is available through a subscription-based license. The data, continually updated for corporate actions and other changes, is delivered to customers on a daily basis, in a choice of file formats including XML and comma-delimited. CounterpartyLink is available through a number of redistributors and distribution channels. See also: Counterparty Link Brochure
