Definition
The Moody's Issuer Number or MIN is a unique 10-digit identifier applied to each issuer of debt rated by Moody's Investor Services.

Additional Information
Moody's ratings track debt covering more than 110 countries, 11,000 corporate issuers, 22,000 public finance issuers, and 94,000 structured finance obligations.
