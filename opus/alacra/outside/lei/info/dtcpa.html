Depositary Trust Corporation Participant Account (DTCPA)

Issued by The Depositary Trust and Clearing Corporation

Type
Issuer

Status
Private

Definition
The Depositary Trust Corporation Participant Account code is a four-digit number used by the Depositary Trust and Clearing Corporation to identify participating depositary institutions.

Additional Information
The listings of participants alphabetically by name and number are available for free to the public, along with the listing of settling banks and depository facilities.
