Definition
The FSA Registration Number or FRA is a unique identifier assigned to every firm, individual or other body regulated by the Financial Service Authority of the United Kingdom.

Additional Information
Firms can only conduct regulated activities in the UK if they are currently authorized by the FSA or registered to do so, or are otherwise exempt. These activities include many of the financial services that consumers and businesses use or deal with each day.
