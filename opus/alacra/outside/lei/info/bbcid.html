Definition
The Bloomberg Company ID is an 8-digit number that uniquely identifies a company on the Bloomberg system.

Additional Information
A Bloomberg Security ID is used in combination with ID_BB_COMPANY in order to distinguish between securities of the same company for Munis, Equities, Corporates, Governments and Preferreds.
