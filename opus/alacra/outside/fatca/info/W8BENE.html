Description
Form W-8BEN-E, Certificate of Foreign Status of Beneficial Owner for United States Tax Withholding (Entities), is a DRAFT form that will be used by foreign entities to certify their non-U.S. status, instead of the current W-8BEN, which will be used only by individuals.
