Description
The FIOD, also known as FIOD-ECD, is the Dutch anti-fraud agency.

Key Facts
The FIOD investigates economic, fiscal and financial fraud. It also has some governance responsibilities in economic and financial areas. The FIOD also carries out research in the field of economic planning and financial integrity. It deals with bankruptcy fraud and anti-money laundering, organized crime and terrorism.
