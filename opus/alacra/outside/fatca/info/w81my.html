Description
Form W-81MY Certificate of Foreign Intermediary, Foreign Flow-Through Entity, or Certain U.S. Branches for United States Tax Withholding is the form that intermediaries and other entities need to submit to withholding agents under the Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act.

Key Facts
It files an annual statement on a form prescribed by the IRS certifying that it satisfies the conditions necessary to be a Qualified Securities Lender.
