Non-Participating Foreign Financial Institution

Type
Entity

Jurisdiction
U.S.

Sponsor/Regulator
U.S. Internal Revenue Service

Description
Non-Participating Foreign Financial Institution (NFFI ) — an FFI that is not a participating FFI, or a Deemed Compliant FFI or otherwise excluded from the application of FATCA. Sometimes called a Bad FFI.
