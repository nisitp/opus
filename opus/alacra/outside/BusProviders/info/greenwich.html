About the Publisher
Greenwich Associates' principal business objective is to provide decision-makers in financial services with expert advice for their most critical strategic issues based upon a unique combination of comprehensive market research and in-depth analysis. Each year, Greenwich meets with up to 40,000 key decision-makers at large corporations, financial services companies, and other institutions in over 70 countries to learn about market trends and their relationships with their providers of financial services.
