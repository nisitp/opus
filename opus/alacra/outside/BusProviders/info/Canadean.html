About the Publisher

Canadean is the industry leading consumer market research company.

They provide global analysis, data and insight through unique, in-depth research and an unrivalled international network of local industry experts. Their primary research capabilities deliver a reach of 4,000,000 consumers worldwide.

Their coverage spans the value chain, from supplier industries such as packaging and ingredients, through to consumer products including food, beverages, alcoholic drinks, health and beauty, as well as retail and out-of-home channels.
