About the Database
TableBase provides access to tabular information on companies, industries, products, countries and markets. Data is drawn from privately published statistical annuals, trade associations, non-profit research groups, government agencies, international organizations, industry reports prepared by investment research groups, and more than 900 publications included in the Business & Industry ® (B&IT) database. Over 20,000 tables a year - with strategic data that is difficult to find elsewhere. Tables from the articles can be downloaded directly to Excel.
