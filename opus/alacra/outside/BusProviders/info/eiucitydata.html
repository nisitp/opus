EIU CityData

About the Database
EIU CityData contains pricing information on over 160 products and services in 123 cities worldwide. Items range from the cost of a loaf of bread to the average electricity bill of a local family, from the cost of office rents to median salary levels. EIU CityData provides the most complete picture of global price levels you can find. See the links below for more detail.
