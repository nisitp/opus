About the Database
MarketLine (formerly Datamonitor), Financial Deals – Identifying and tracking major financial activity across our full scope of company and sector coverage, MarketLine Financial Deals covers mergers & acquisitions, private equity/venture capital backed buyouts, investments, and exits, IPOs and secondary and follow on offerings, and private placements and exits. Additional non-corporate financial deals including partnership agreements, joint ventures, co-development and research, licensing and co-marketing agreements are also available.
