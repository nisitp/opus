About the Database
A comprehensive listing of directors and select officers of companies traded on the NYSE, NASDAQ and AMEX stock exchanges. To compile this information, Equilar's staff of data analysts researches SEC filings, company websites, press releases, and most importantly, utilizes company and director self-reported information.

About the Publisher

Equilar, formerly Corporate Board Member, an NYSE Euronext company, is the leading information and education resource for senior officers and directors of publicly traded corporations, large private companies, and Global 1000 firms. The quarterly publication, Corporate Board Member magazine, provides readers with decision-making tools to deal with the strategic and corporate governance challenges confronting their boards. Corporate Board Member further extends its governance leadership through an online resource center, conferences, roundtables, and timely research. The magazine maintains the most comprehensive, up-to-date database of directors and officers serving on boards of publicly traded companies listed with NYSE Euronext and The NASDAQ OMX Group Inc stock exchanges.
