About the Publisher
Vickers provides the most complete and comprehensive picture of institutional and insider shareholding positions available. With Vickers Stock Research, you can download Institutional Equity and Fixed Income Ownership data, 5% Equity Ownership data, and Insider Equity Ownership data. The single security report provides information from a variety of sources including 13F Filings, voluntary filings, share registers, 13D&G, forms 3, 4 and 144.
