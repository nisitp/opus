About the Publisher
Hemscott plc is a leading business & financial information provider which has been established since 1985. Hemscott Company Reports offer business and financial information on UK companies through two types of reports. Company Fact Sheets provide business details, share data, valuation metrics, key statistics and dates, progress reports, major shareholders, directors, regulatory news, as well as five-year price charts, trading records and forecasts. Company Insight Reports, in addition to all the inform ation in the Fact Sheets, also supply detailed and consensus broker forecasts, ROCE graphs, EPS charts, P/E ratio charts, operating margins graphs, executive/non-executive directors, share dealings, P&L accounts and five year balance sheets with notes.
