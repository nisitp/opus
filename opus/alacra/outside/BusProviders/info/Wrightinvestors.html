About the Publisher
Wright Investors' Service, an internationally recognized investment advisory firm, produces timely, in-depth research reports on over 31,000 of the world's leading public companies. Wright Industry Averages are a series of reports that have been compiled from the data of the underlying companies. Included for each industry (for a six-year period) are income statements, balance sheets, sources of capital report, sales analysis, valuation data, ratio analyses reports and industry leaders ranked by sales, total assets and market capitalization.
