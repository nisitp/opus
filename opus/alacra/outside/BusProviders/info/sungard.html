About the Publisher
SunGard Market Data Services, an operating group of SunGard (NYSE: SDS), is a market data aggregator, distributor, and wholesaler for the financial services industry, providing market data delivery solutions that serve a wide range of investment applications. With connections to major exchanges worldwide, SunGard Market Data Services maintains a deep historical data set, aggregated financial content from over 100 third-party sources, and a resilient, fully redundant infrastructure.
