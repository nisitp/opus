About the Database
A global database comprising over 7.25 million company documents, delivering International Filings and SEC Filings. Content includes Global Annual and Interim Reports, U.S. Annuals and Paper Filings, SEC Filings, Shareholder Filings, Global Bond Prospectuses, Global M & A documents and Company News. Perfect Filings provides an in-depth search engine where a user can search for filings using over 3,000 indexed classifications. Other key search options include company, country, classification, industry and document type.
