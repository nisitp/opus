About the Publisher
The Freedonia Group is a leading international business research firm that publishes approximately 250 Focus reports. Freedonia Focus provides insights into over eighteen industry groups. Each report packs Freedonia's industry outlook in close to twenty pages of crisp, highly focused analysis. These reports provide a wealth of industry insights targeting market size, product and market segmentation, product and market forecasts, industry composition, market leaders and business trends.
