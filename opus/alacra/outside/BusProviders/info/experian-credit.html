About the Publisher
Experian is a leading provider of global commercial information and images to organisations worldwide. As experts in the provision of UK data, we offer coverage for approximately 1.6 million Limited companies. Our online reports offer in-depth information which includes mortgages and corporate structures, as well as Directors and Secretaries. In addition, we offer a wide range of imaged documents including Accounts, Annual returns, Directors (Forms 288), Capital documents, Incorporation and Change of Name documents.
