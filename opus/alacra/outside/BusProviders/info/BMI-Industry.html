About the Publisher
BMI's customers and clients span more than 140 countries worldwide, including more than 400 of the Global Fortune 500 companies. Businesses, banks, financial service companies, governments, academia and research centres have all come to rely on BMI's analysis, data and forecasts – and have done so for 25 years. The company was awarded the Queen's Award for Export Achievement in 1997. BMI offers a comprehensive range of products and services designed to help senior executives, analysts and researchers assess and better manage operating risks, and exploit business opportunities, across 175 markets.
