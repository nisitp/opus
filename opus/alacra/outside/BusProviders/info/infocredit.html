About the Publisher
InfoCredit Group provides reports that present a current, objective picture of how a company manages its financial obligations, providing an unbiased view of a company's overall financial health. Reports include name, legal status, and registration number, full contact details of a company, and a credit rating based upon the content of the report. The universe of coverage includes the Middle East, Cyprus, Malta and Romania
