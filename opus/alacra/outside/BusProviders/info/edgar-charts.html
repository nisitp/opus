About the Publisher
Edgar Online offers various stock price and performance chart options for over 43,000 North American and over 41,000 international securities. Coverage includes the major stock exchanges, Pink Sheets, OTCBB, and Mutual Funds in North America and history goes back to 1990. For international securities Edgar Online covers the principle stock exchanges in Australia, Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Norway, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom, India, China, Hong Kong, South Korea, Qatar, Isreal and Singapore. International history generally goes back to 1995. Charts with intraday prices are available for US securities, while end-of-day charts are available for non-US securities. Charting options include index overlays, upper indications of events such as stock splits and dividends, as well as various lower studies tracking volume, momentum, and money flow.
